🚀 Getting Started

Welcome to Leadership Notes!

Leadership Notes helps supervisors, managers, and team leaders maintain professional documentation of employee coaching conversations and workplace incidents.

Your First Steps

  1. Set Up Teams - Go to Settings → Teams to create your teams
  2. Add Team Members - Tap Team tab and add your employees
  3. Customize Categories - Settings → Categories to adjust incident types
  4. Create Your First Entry - Tap Entry tab and document your first incident

🔧 Troubleshooting

Paywall / Subscription Issues

Problem: Can't tap "Continue" button on paywall

Solution:

  1. Force quit the app (swipe up from app switcher)
  2. Reopen Leadership Notes
  3. If issue persists, restart your iPhone
  4. Check your internet connection

Problem: "Restore Purchases" doesn't find subscription

Solution:

  1. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID
  2. Check Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions to verify
  3. Try tapping "Restore Purchases" again after a few minutes

Report Issues

Problem: PDF report won't generate

Solution:

  1. Make sure you have entries in the selected date range
  2. Try a shorter date range
  3. Check available storage space on your device
  4. Restart the app and try again

Data & Sync Issues

Problem: Data disappeared after update

Solution:

Your data is stored locally on your device. It should not disappear with updates. If this happens:
  1. Check if you're logged into the same device
  2. Restart your device
  3. Contact support immediately (see below)

Navigation Issues

Problem: Tab bar not responding

Solution:

  1. Tap more firmly on the tab icons
  2. Make sure you're tapping the icon/label, not between buttons
  3. Restart the app if issue persists

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Leadership Notes cost?

Leadership Notes is $0.99/month after a 7-day free trial. You can cancel anytime with no commitment.

Can I use Leadership Notes on multiple devices?

Your subscription works on any device signed into your Apple ID. However, data does NOT sync between devices - each device maintains its own local records.

Is there a limit to how many entries or team members I can add?

No! Add unlimited entries and unlimited team members.

Can I export my data?

Yes! Generate PDF reports (Reports tab → Share Report) or create full backups (Settings → Data → Backup).

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period. After that, you'll need to resubscribe to access the app. Your data remains on your device.

Can I customize categories?

Yes! Go to Settings → Categories to add, edit, or delete incident categories.

How do I delete a team member?

Go to Team tab → tap the team member → scroll down → tap "Delete [Name] & All Entries". This will permanently delete them and all their entries.

Can I edit or delete old entries?

Yes! Go to History → tap an entry to expand → tap "Edit" or tap the trash icon to delete.

How do follow-up reminders work?

When creating an entry, enable "Follow-up" and choose a timeframe (24 hours, 48 hours, etc.). You'll see upcoming reminders on the Entry tab.

Is my data backed up to iCloud?

No, Leadership Notes does NOT use iCloud. All data stays locally on your device only. You must manually create backups (Settings → Data → Backup) to save your data.
