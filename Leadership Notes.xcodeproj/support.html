‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is my data backed up to the cloud?
No. All data is stored locally on your device only. Leadership Notes does not use any cloud services, servers, or external storage. We recommend using the Export feature regularly to create manual backups.
Q: Can I sync between multiple devices?
Not currently. Since all data is stored locally for privacy, there is no automatic sync. You can export your data from one device and import it on another using the Backup/Import features in Settings ‚Üí Data.
Q: What happens if I delete the app?
All your data will be permanently deleted. Make sure to export a backup before uninstalling if you want to keep your data.
Q: How do I backup my data?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Data ‚Üí Backup. This exports all your data as a JSON file that you can save to Files, email to yourself, or store in iCloud Drive.
Q: How do I restore from a backup?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Data ‚Üí Import. Select your backup JSON file. This will merge the backup data with any existing entries (duplicates are avoided by ID).
Q: Can I use this on iPad?
Yes! Leadership Notes is a universal app that works on both iPhone and iPad running iOS/iPadOS 17.0 or later.
Q: Do I need a subscription?
Leadership Notes offers a 7-day free trial, then $0.99/month. The subscription provides access to all features including unlimited entries, reports, and backups.
Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
Open the Settings app on your device ‚Üí Tap your name at the top ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí Select Leadership Notes ‚Üí Cancel Subscription. Your subscription remains active until the end of the billing period.
Q: Can I get a refund?
All purchases are handled by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com or contact Apple Support directly.

Q: How do reminders work?
You can set reminders for important dates (birthdays, certifications) in the People tab. You can also add follow-up reminders when creating entries. Reminders appear at the top of the Entry screen.

üîß Troubleshooting

App crashes or freezes

Try these steps:

  1. Force quit the app (swipe up from bottom and swipe up on the app)
  2. Restart your device
  3. Make sure you're running the latest version of the app
  4. Check that you're on iOS/iPadOS 17.0 or later
  5. If the issue persists, contact support

Data not saving

If your entries aren't being saved:

  1. Make sure you have enough storage space on your device
  2. Check that the app has proper permissions (Settings ‚Üí Leadership Notes)
  3. Try exporting a backup, then reinstalling the app
  4. Import your backup after reinstalling

Can't send email reports

To use the email report feature:

  1. Make sure you have the Mail app configured on your device
  2. Go to Settings ‚Üí Mail ‚Üí Accounts and add an email account
  3. If you use a third-party email app, you can use the "Share" option instead

Subscription not working

If you've purchased but features aren't unlocking:

  1. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase
  2. Try restoring purchases (if available in app settings)
  3. Close and reopen the app
  4. Check your App Store purchase history to confirm the purchase went through

üéØ Best Practices

Documentation Tips

  • Be timely: Log entries as soon as possible after events occur
  • Be specific: Include dates, times, and concrete details
  • Be objective: Focus on observable behaviors and facts
  • Be consistent: Use the same categories and formats for similar situations
  • Use notes: Add context that might be helpful later

Data Management

  • Weekly: Review your entries and follow-ups
  • Monthly: Generate reports for your records
  • Quarterly: Export a backup
  • Yearly: Use the Archive feature to email and remove old entries

Privacy & Security

  • Keep your device password-protected
  • Enable Face ID or Touch ID for additional security
  • Don't share your device with others
  • Store backup files securely
  • Be mindful of what you write (it stays on your device, but could be seen if someone accesses your phone)
